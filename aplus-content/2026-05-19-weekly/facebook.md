Summer is almost here. If your child was a little behind in math or reading this year, the next 8 weeks matter more than you might think.

Three questions worth asking your school this week:

1. Did my child meet their spring growth goals in math and reading?
2. If not, what is the school's plan for the summer?
3. Will my child have the same teacher or tutor across the whole summer block?

Same tutor, every session, with a clear plan for what to work on. That is what moves the needle. Rotating teachers and worksheets do not.

If your school does not have a plan yet, you still have time. The right summer program can help your child walk into next year inside grade level, not further behind.
