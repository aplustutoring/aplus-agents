Summer is almost here. If your child fell a little behind in math or reading this year, the next 8 weeks are more important than they look.

The kids who keep moving over the summer are the ones working with a consistent person, on the right material, for enough time. Not summer school. Not random worksheets. A clear plan, the same tutor each session, 3-4 times a week, until school starts again.

If your school does not have a summer plan yet, the next two weeks are when those decisions get made. Ask your school three questions this week:

1. Did my child meet their spring growth goals?
2. What is the school's summer support plan?
3. Will the same tutor work with my child the whole summer?
