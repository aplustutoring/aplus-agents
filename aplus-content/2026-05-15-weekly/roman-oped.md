**Anchor slice from blog:** Section "Why this matters for charter directors." The system-level read on why categorical-grant dependence was always a planning risk.
**Target word count:** 100 (90-110)

---

Federal grants were never the foundation.

The schools surviving the 2026 procurement cycle didn't suddenly get smart this spring. They were never planning around the assumption that all categorical federal dollars would flow on schedule. They built around Title I, IDEA, and the state layers because that's the math that worked in 2019 too.

The directors panicking right now are the ones who treated stimulus and categorical grants as recurring revenue. That was always a temporary frame.

The intervention math doesn't change. The funding mix does.
