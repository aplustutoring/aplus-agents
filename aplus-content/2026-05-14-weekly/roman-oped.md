# Roman Op-Ed: Title III Deadline

**Channel:** Roman Slavinsky personal LinkedIn (linkedin.com/in/romanslavinsky)

**Target word count:** 100 (90-110 acceptable)

We're going to send federal money back to the state. Again.

September 30, 2026 is the obligation deadline for 2024-25 Title III funds. After that, anything not contractually committed reverts to CDE. Charter LEAs across California are sitting on Title III balances no one has time to obligate, because the principal supposed to route them is doing four other jobs.

This is what "underfunded" actually means in practice. The dollars are there. The system supposed to deploy them isn't.

The kids who needed that intervention don't get a second year. They age.
