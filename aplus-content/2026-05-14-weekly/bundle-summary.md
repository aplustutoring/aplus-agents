## What this week's anchor is arguing and who it is for

The blog argues that California charter LEAs are sitting on Title III English Learner and Immigrant funds that revert to the state on September 30, 2026 if not obligated, and that the structural reasons charter schools underspend (small business offices, hard vendor identification under supplemental-not-supplant, audit-risk avoidance) are exactly the reasons a vendor like A+ Tutoring exists. It is written for charter directors, special programs coordinators, and intervention coordinators who are responsible for routing categorical funds but have other full-time jobs. The piece doubles as Danielle's sales asset she can link to in pitch emails and as a permanent SEO asset that ranks for "Title III funding deadline California charter schools" over time.
