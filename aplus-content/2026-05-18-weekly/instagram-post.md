If your child attends a California charter school, there's something coming this fall worth knowing about.

The 2026 California School Dashboard arrives in November. It tells the state which schools need extra federal support for the next three years. Any school identified gets put on an improvement plan.

If your school could be on the list, the leadership is already thinking about what intervention services your child might be offered. Now's the time to ask.

Three questions worth bringing up this spring:
What did our school's 2025 Dashboard look like?
What's the plan if we're identified this fall?
What kind of tutoring or support would my child be eligible for?

Schools running real programs already will have clear answers. The ones that hesitate are worth a follow-up.
