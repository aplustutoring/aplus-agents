Has your child ever come home and said "reading is hard" and shrugged it off?

This fall, most California schools will start screening every K, 1st, and 2nd grader for early signs of dyslexia. The same is true in many other states. If your child gets flagged, your school is supposed to have a clear plan.

Three questions worth asking your school this summer:

1. Will my child be screened for dyslexia this fall?
2. If my child is flagged, what is the intervention plan?
3. Will my child work with the same tutor or specialist all year?

Catching it early changes everything. Waiting until third grade does not.

If you have a child in kindergarten, first, or second grade, save this post and ask your school these questions before September.
